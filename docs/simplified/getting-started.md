## What is this app?

This is a tool that helps you manage a fish farm. Think of it like a digital notebook that keeps track of everything happening on the farm - from fish tanks to inventory supplies!

## Who can use it?

- **Farm Workers** - People who feed fish, check water, and do daily tasks
- **Managers** - People who oversee operations and check reports
- **Admins** - People who control who can access what in the app

## How to Get Started

### Step 1: Log In
1. Open the app in your browser
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"

### Step 2: Check the Dashboard
- Look at the main page to see what needs attention
- Red alerts = urgent, needs immediate action
- Yellow warnings = keep an eye on this
- Green = everything is good!

### Step 3: Navigate Around
Use the menu on the left side to go to different sections:
- Click **Dashboard** to return home
- Click **Inventory** to check supplies
- Click **Biofloc** to work with fish tanks
- Click **Admin** to manage users (if you're an admin)
